Team TMC · New Leader Intake · 2026

Getting to know
who you are.

No right or wrong answers here. The more honest you are, the better Rico can lead you. This is about building real trust from day one.

Rep Info
1

Background & Journey

The shape of how you got here.

1
Walk me through your career so far. What led you to sales, and what led you to Okta?
2
What is the highlight of your career so far? What are you most proud of?
3
What is something about you outside of work that would help me understand you better?
2

What Drives You

Not the polished answer — the real one.

4
What actually motivates you? Money, recognition, competition, mastery, freedom, something else?
5
When you are having your best day at work, what does that look like?
6
What are your financial or lifestyle goals this year? Where do you want to be in three?
7
Do you want my job someday, or a different path? Be honest — it changes how I invest in you.
3

How You Work Best

Everyone is different. Tell me yours.

8
How do you prefer to be coached? In the moment, scheduled, shadowing, challenge, something else?
9
How do you like to receive tough feedback?
10
How do you like to be recognized when you win? Public, private, tangible, verbal?
11
What is your preferred cadence and format for 1:1s? What makes them valuable to you?
12
What communication channel works best day to day: Slack, text, email, phone?
4

What You Need From Me

I will take this seriously.

13
Describe the best leader you have worked for. What did they do that you want me to do?
14
Describe the worst leader you have worked for. What should I never do?
15
What do you need from me in the first 90 days to feel supported?
16
Where do you want me to stay out of your way?
5

The Business

You know this territory. I am still learning it.

17
What is working right now that we should protect and double down on?
18
What is broken or frustrating about how we operate that I should know about?
19
Where are you strongest in your sales motion, and where do you know you need to grow?
20
What is the single biggest obstacle keeping you from crushing quota right now?
21
If you had a magic wand and could change one thing about your territory, tools, or process, what would it be?
6

Trust & Transparency

One last pair of questions.

22
Is there anything going on personally or professionally I should be aware of so I can support you?
23
What is one question you have for me that I should answer honestly?
